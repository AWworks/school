Welcome

KIAS (Knowledge International Arabic School), established in 2014 is a turning point where we hope to create a center of learning with an ethos where young Muslims can receive a broad and balanced education. The name KNOWLEDGE itself reflects our goals and motto. It is established to cater the needs of Islamic and formal education.

KIAS is a unique school imparting quality education in an Islamic environment. The gateway to good life is through good education. Our school honours and propagates the principles of Islam and works towards student acquisition of knowledge bestowed in Qur'an and Hadeeth.
